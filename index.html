About

I’m Pa’O ethnic, born in HsiHseng, South Shan State, Myanmar. My interest in Android started in 2014 when I got my first phone — an Acer Liquid Z3 — while working in Bangkok as a painting worker. Back then, rooting was the only way to install Zawgyi Myanmar font on Android, and that curiosity eventually led me to build zFont in 2018 — my most successful project to date. It pushed me deep into Android reverse engineering and finding ways to bypass font restrictions imposed by OEMs. Today I develop Kotlin and Flutter apps — font tools, file managers, ADB utilities, and language apps for the Pa’O community. Kotlin is my primary language; I also work with Dart, JavaScript, Python, and Bash.
